How To Play

  • Rule 1:

    is a trivia game. Each player starts with 500 points.

  • Rule 2:

    When it's your turn you'll be shown a category.

  • Rule 3:

    You can decide to answer the question yourself or you can force another player to answer.

  • Rule 4:

    If you keep the question and answer correctly, you get nothing!

  • Rule 5:

    If you get it wrong, you'll lose 100 points.

  • Rule 6:

    If you pass the question and the player you pass to gets it right,you'll lose 200 points.

  • Rule 7:

    If they get it wrong they will lose 100 points.

  • Rule 8:

    There is no way to get points back.

  • Rule 9:

    Play continues until one player loses all of their points or until the question pool for that game has been exhausted.

  • Rule 10:

    There are no winners.

  • Rule 11:

    The object of the game is to not be the one who loses.

  • Rule 12:

    Are you the least intelligent person in the room? Time to find out!
